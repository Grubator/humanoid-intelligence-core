# Galaxy

## Purpose

Stores galaxies where humanoids have been confirmed.

## Required Fields

- Galaxy Name
- Dominant Species
- Number of Humanoids
- Faster-than-light Travel
- Beer Availability
- Probability of Meeting R2-D2

## Optional Fields

- Death Star Present
- Imperial Occupation
- Wookie Population
- Pan Galactic Gargle Blaster Rating

## Example

Galaxy Name: Milky Way

Dominant Species: Humans (still arguing)

Number of Humanoids: Growing...

Beer Availability: Excellent

Probability of Meeting R2-D2: Unfortunately close to zero.
