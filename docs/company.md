# Company

## Purpose

Represents companies participating in the humanoid robotics ecosystem that are not primarily humanoid manufacturers.

## Required Fields

- Company Name
- Industry
- Headquarters
- Country
- Founded
- Website
- Description
- Related Technologies
- Related Robots
- Related Manufacturers

## Optional Fields

- Parent Company
- Stock Ticker
- Number of Employees
- Annual Revenue
- Notes

## Example

Company Name: NVIDIA

Industry: Artificial Intelligence / Semiconductors

Headquarters: Santa Clara, California, USA

Founded: 1993

Website: https://www.nvidia.com

Description:
Provides GPUs and AI computing platforms used for humanoid robotics.

Related Technologies:
- Isaac
- CUDA
- Jetson
