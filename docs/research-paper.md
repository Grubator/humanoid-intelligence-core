# Research Paper

## Purpose
Stores scientific publications related to humanoid robotics.

## Required Fields

- Title
- Authors
- Publication Year
- Journal / Conference
- DOI
- URL
- Abstract
- Keywords
- Related Technologies
- Related Robots
- Related Manufacturers

## Optional Fields

- PDF
- BibTeX Citation
- Notes
